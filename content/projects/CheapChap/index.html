CheapChap finds cheap and affordable date solutions for you and your date. You date may not be happy, but your wallet will be! My friends, Jarek Sedlacek, Wayne Sun and Russell Frank, and I developed this application during HackNY, a 24 hour hackathon in October 2011. The objective of the hackaton was to create a product wrapped around one or more APIs presented at the hackaton. 50% was graded in the idea and 50% was graded on deliverable. We built the application using Node.js, Express.js, Jade, Stylus, MongoDB and of course jQuery. https://github.com/russfrank/hacknyf2011
