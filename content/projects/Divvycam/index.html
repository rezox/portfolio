Divvycam is can iOS application which allows users to easily share their photos between their devices. This application started off as a concept idea then was later developed due to growing interest. Divvycam is built using Titanium, Amazon AWS, Amazon S3, Lighttpd, PHP and MySQL. More information can be found at: http://divvy.burst-dev.com You can also try the app yourself at: http://itunes.apple.com/us/app/divvycam/id480270517?ls=1&mt=8
