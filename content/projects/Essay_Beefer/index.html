Paste your essay into the box to beef it up. Our specially trained cows will automagically find sources, insert quotes, citations, and filler statements as well as building a bibliography and finding images! Essay Beefer was developed during HackNY Spring 2012 by myself, Russell Frank, Wayne Sun, and Jarek Sedlacek. https://github.com/sjlu/hacknys2012.git http://beef.stevenlu.com
